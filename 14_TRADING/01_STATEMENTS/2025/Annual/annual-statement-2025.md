## Glossary

Price

The end-of-day market price in the instrument currency.


Profit



Corrections made to the account balance due to unusual circumstances (e.g. changes to the amount of a corporate

action after distribution)


Average Price

The average price of the open prices for each fill for the position.


Bonus

Free shares, received as part of a Trading 212 promotion.


CFD

Contract for difference.


Dividend

In the CFD Overview, it shows the net dividend received.


Dividend adjustment

When an adjustment related to an already-paid dividend is processed.


Net Dividend

Dividend received following withholding taxation in an Invest account.


Gross Dividend

Dividend received prior to withholding taxation in an Invest account.


Loss

The realized negative result from trading via a CFD or Invest account.


Execution price

The price at which the instrument was sold or a position was closed.


FX Fee

Represents the total amount of currency conversion fees from your trades and manual currency exchanges.
