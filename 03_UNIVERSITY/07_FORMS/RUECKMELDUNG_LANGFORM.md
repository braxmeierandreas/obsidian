# **Info on re-registration**

Student Affairs Department


Furtwangen, 15.09.2025/flh/tai

**Re-registration for winter semester 25/26 starts on 7 July 2025.**
**Please transfer your semester fee by 15 August 2025 at the latest, so that you will be re-registered by start**
**of winter semester.**

It may take up to 8 days for the transfer, as they are forwarded to the universities via the Landesoberkasse. Reregistration takes place automatically as soon as your semester fees have been completely received by HFU. You
can then create new study certificates in MIO.
Late re-registration will result in a late fee of €10 and a reminder procedure with the threat of de-registration.

**Semester fee 176,- €**
(Exceptions apply to guest students who are enrolled at HFU as part of an agreement).

The semester fee must also be transferred during the **practical semester, study semester abroad** and the **semester of**

The tuition fees for **international students** (€ 1500) and for a **second degree** (€ 650) must also be transferred on time.
During the practical semester and the semester of leave of absence, students are exempt from the tuition fees for

If you have **completed all examinations** and coursework by the end of the summer semester 2025 (till 31.08.2025) and
