application for leave may be possible if submitted no later than the end of the course enrolment period ( _Ende der Belegungszeit_ ); the application has to be submitted
immediately upon the occurrence of the reason for leave however.


Note: Study and Examination Regulations (SPO) – General Section, § 31 (for Bachelor’s Degree Programmes) and § 24a (for Master’s Degree Programmes):
“Students on leave of absence are not allowed to participate in any kind of examination (see § 61section 3 of the State University Regulations (LHG) for exceptions
relating to the maternity rights law). Due to an extension of the submission date, work begun in a previous semester may be submitted for completion in the following
semesters.“


Note: State University Regulations (LHG), § 61 Section. 1: „Upon application, students may, for a good reason be freed from their duty to attend to their studies (leave
of absence). Generally, the duration of such a leave of absence should be no longer than two semesters.


Note for students from non-EU countries with a residence permit for study purposes: Make sure you contact the Immigration Authorities prior to applying for a leave of
absence as it may lead to a revocation of your residence permit and therefore a forced-registration!!
