_The Bachelor thesis topic is assigned following the fifth taught semester at earliest, and no later than three months after the completion of all other assessments. (§ 24 (1),_
_SPO). The faculty’s implementation regulations for theses [as contained in the SPO’s Specific Section] will define any additional pre-conditions for admission to the_
_Bachelor thesis._















_Please note: The bachelor thesis registration has to be submitted to the faculty office within the re-registration period (normally 1st September or 1st March). A late_
_registration is subject to charges._











_Please note: Normally, the start date (start of the lead time) is 1st March for SoSe / 1st September WiSe. Submission deadline (four months after the start date) is_
_normally 30th June for SoSe / 31st December for WiSe. In case of extension (no more than two months) the submission deadline will normally be 31st August /_
_28th /29th February. The thesis has to be submitted to the relevant faculty office. A thesis which has not been submitted within the deadline will be graded as not_
_sufficient (5,0)._
