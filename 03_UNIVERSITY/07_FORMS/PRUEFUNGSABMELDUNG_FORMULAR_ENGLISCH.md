**Please note** (extracts of study and examination regulations for bachelor programs, article 11 and for master programs article
10):
Before the **first registration** for an exam, the student can withdraw from such exam without having to give a reason. Such an
application has to be filed with the examinations office in writing **no later than day prior to the exam** . If an exam consists of
more than one part a postponement is only possible, before sitting the first part of the exam. In these cases the postponement
must be submitted no later then the day prior the first part of the exam.

If the exam is missed without an application for postponement in due time or without cause the exam will be graded “notadequate” (5,0). The reason claimed for non-attendance or absence must be displayed (in writing) and substantiated
immediately (e. g. submission of medical certificate).

**Postponement of exams specified on this form are legally binding.**

**Recipient of BAföG – Please note: Voluntary postponement can cause loss to BAföG-claim.**
