Virtual reality simulations (VRS) in nursing education typically promote independent learning with limited faculty involvement. This study investigated how embedding clinical instructors within VR environments influences nursing learners' experiences. This descriptive qualitative study implemented a novel Virtual Reality Simulation Embedded Instructor Model with junior nursing students (n = 213) across two semesters.
